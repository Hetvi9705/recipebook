    Creamy Chicken Alfredo

    Published: November 15, 2025 Author: Hetvi Patel

    Description

    Indulge in this restaurant-quality Chicken Alfredo made right at home. Juicy chicken, al dente pasta, and a velvety Parmesan cream sauce come together for an unforgettable meal.

    Recipe Details

    This Chicken Alfredo recipe features tender chicken breast and perfectly cooked fettuccine coated in a rich, creamy Parmesan sauce. It's comfort food at its finest and easier to make than you think!

    Ingredients

    • 1 lb fettuccine pasta
    • 2 chicken breasts, cut into strips
    • 4 tablespoons butter
    • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 cups heavy cream
    • 1½ cups grated Parmesan cheese
    • Salt and black pepper
    • Fresh parsley for garnish
    • Olive oil for cooking
